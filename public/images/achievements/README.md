# Achievement Photos

Add your achievement photos here. They should be named:

- achievement-1.jpg  (First Hackathon Win)
- achievement-2.jpg  (Herbal Garden Launch)
- achievement-3.jpg  (Technical Seminar Lead)
- achievement-4.jpg  (Internship Milestone)
- achievement-5.jpg  (Open Source Contribution)
- achievement-6.jpg  (Project Showcase Award)

## Recommended specs
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: at least 320×230px
- Aspect ratio: ~4:3 or 3:2 works best
- The photos are displayed with a warm sepia tone overlay

## Customising captions
Edit `components/chapters/ChapterAchievements.tsx` → the `FRAMES` array at the top.
Each entry has: `src`, `caption`, and `year`.

## If no images are added
The chapter shows elegant placeholder slots inviting you to add your photos.
Everything else works normally.
